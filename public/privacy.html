Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

ZhenMing ("we", "our", "the App") collects minimal information necessary to provide our I Ching divination service:

  • Divination queries: The questions you submit for analysis are processed to generate results. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Device storage: Your divination history is saved in your device's local storage (IndexedDB/localStorage) and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use cloud sync features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate divination analysis results based on your questions
  • To save your divination history on your device for your convenience
  • To improve the quality of our analysis service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your divination data is primarily stored on your device. When analysis requires server processing, your question and hexagram data are sent to our server via encrypted HTTPS connection. We do not permanently store your queries on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party AI services (such as Alibaba Cloud Qianwen) to enhance analysis quality. When these services are used, your query content is sent to the respective provider. Please refer to their privacy policies for details on how they handle data.

5. Data Retention

Local data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. You can clear all data through the App's settings at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and export your divination history
  • Delete all stored data through the App's settings
  • Opt out of cloud sync features

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: zhenming@example.com

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年2月

1. 我们收集的信息

贞明（"我们"、"本应用"）仅收集提供六爻解卦服务所必需的最少信息：

  • 占卜问题：您提交的问题用于生成分析结果，这些数据存储在您的设备本地。
  • 设备存储：您的占卜历史保存在设备本地存储中（IndexedDB/localStorage），除非您主动使用云同步功能，否则不会传输到我们的服务器。

2. 我们如何使用您的信息

  • 根据您的问题生成占卜分析结果
  • 在您的设备上保存占卜历史，方便您查看
  • 提升分析服务质量

3. 数据存储与安全

您的占卜数据主要存储在您的设备上。当分析需要服务器处理时，您的问题和卦象数据通过加密的 HTTPS 连接发送到我们的服务器。我们不会在服务器上永久存储您的查询内容。

4. 第三方服务

本应用可能使用第三方 AI 服务（如阿里云通义千问）来提升分析质量。使用这些服务时，您的查询内容会发送给相应的服务提供商。请参阅其隐私政策了解数据处理详情。

5. 数据保留

本地数据保留在您的设备上，直到您选择删除。您可以随时通过应用设置清除所有数据。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 您的权利

  • 访问并导出您的占卜历史
  • 通过应用设置删除所有存储数据
  • 选择退出云同步功能

8. 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有变更，我们会在应用内发布新版政策通知您。

9. 联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系我们：zhenming@example.com
